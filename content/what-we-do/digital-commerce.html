MOBILIZING THE STORE

Mobility is revolutionizing the in-store experience.

Whether it’s a mobile POS, kiosk or clienteling, there’s a growing trend toward using mobile devices in-store to provide a more interactive shopping experience. We’re helping sales associates deliver a highly personalized in-store buying experience with the mobile tools to help customers make purchasing decisions.

MOBILIZING CATALOGS & CALL CENTERS

Driven by mobile devices, the world of paper catalogs continues to evolve.

Mobile commerce apps provide access to call centers at the touch of a button, allowing users to place orders and ask questions. We enable retailers to take advantage of data collected by mobile devices to track the effectiveness of marketing activities like physical catalogs, billboards and advertising.
